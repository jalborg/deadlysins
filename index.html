1. RELIABLE

01. RELIABLE

A reliable behavior is based on a person who are able to be believed by others and likely to be true or correct. According to the study, 95% of the participants saw themself having this quality and 49% saw this in people in their surroundings.

2. CONSIDERATE

02. CONSIDERATE

Considerate is the behavior of taking everyone in to consideration or caring about the large amount. According to the study, 94% of the participants saw themself having this quality and 46% saw this in people in their surroundings.

3. HUMBLE

03. HUMBLE

A Humble person is someone who is peaceful and knows his/her boundaries. According to the study, 96% of the participants saw themself having this quality and 41% saw this in people in their surroundings.

4. COMPASSIONATE

04. COMPASSIONATE

Compassionate is defined by feeling or showing concern for other people. According to the study, 97% of the participants saw themself having this quality and 45% saw this in people in their surroundings.

5. TOLERANT

05. TOLERANT

Tolerance is the definition when a person have the patient and understanding of others. According to the study, 95% of the participants saw themself having this quality and 43% saw this in people in their surroundings.

6. GENEROUS

06. GENEROUS

Generous is defined by a behavior where a person is showing kindness and concern for others. According to the study, 95% of the participants saw themself having this quality and 43% saw this in people in their surroundings.

07. ENGAGED

A significant behavior that is shown through a person by promises and dedication in what they are doing. According to the study, 95% of the participants saw themself having this quality and 43% saw this in people in their surroundings.

7. GREEDY

07. GREEDY

Greedy is defined by a person having or showing a selfish desire for wealth and possessions, such as money or food. According to the study, 57% of the participants saw themself having this quality and 17% saw this in people in their surroundings.

6. XENOPHOBIC

06. XENOPHOBIC

Is defined by a person who is unduly fearful of what is foreign and especially of people of foreign origin. According to the study, 47% of the participants saw themself having this quality and 18% saw this in people in their surroundings.

5. NARROW-MINDED

05. NARROW-MINDED

A narrow-minded behavior is defined by a person with a lack in tolerance and are not willing to accept opinions, beliefs, or behaviors that are unusual or different from your own. According to the study, 53% of the participants saw themself having this quality and 18% saw this in people in their surroundings.

4. BULLY

04. BULLY

Bully is used as a word that describes someone who frightens, hurts, or threatens smaller or weaker people. According to the study, 32% of the participants saw themself having this quality and 13% saw this in people in their surroundings.

3. RUTHLESS

03. RUTHLESS

Ruthless is defined as a person who have no pity and have a cruel or merciless behavior. According to the study, 38% of the participants saw themself having this quality and 12% saw this in people in their surroundings.

2. HATEFUL

02. HATEFUL

Hateful is defined by a person showing a very bad or evil behavior. According to the study, 52% of the participants saw themself having this quality and 11% saw this in people in their surroundings.

1. FAKE

01. FAKE

A fake behavior is defined as a person who pretends to have some special knowledge or ability or pretends to be someone else. According to the study, 39% of the participants saw themself having this quality and 17% saw this in people in their surroundings.
